Rice Mill Machine – Product Overview

Rice Mill Machines from Swarnabha Industries are designed for complete rice processing from paddy to polished rice. Our machines handle cleaning, de-husking, separation, polishing, grading, and packaging. Built for Indian rice varieties, these machines deliver high yield with minimal broken rice. Whether you are operating a small commercial rice mill or a large-scale processing plant, our robust and efficient machines ensure consistent output quality with low maintenance requirements. Each system is engineered for Indian paddy conditions and can be customized to handle specific rice varieties and capacities.

Key Features

  • Complete rice processing line from paddy to polished rice
  • Multi-stage cleaning system
  • High milling yield with minimal wastage
  • Automatic husk separation
  • Rice polishing & grading system
  • Low broken rice ratio
