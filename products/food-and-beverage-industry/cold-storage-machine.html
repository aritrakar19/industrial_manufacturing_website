Cold Storage Machine – Product Overview

Cold Storage Machines from Swarnabha Industries provide reliable temperature-controlled storage for perishable goods. From walk-in cold rooms to large industrial cold storage, our solutions maintain optimal temperatures for fruits, vegetables, dairy, meat, seafood, and pharmaceutical products. Engineered with energy-efficient compressors, PUF insulated panels, and digital monitoring systems, our cold storage units ensure product freshness and extended shelf life. Whether you need a single-chamber unit or a multi-zone facility, we design and deliver solutions tailored to your specific storage requirements.

Key Features

  • Precision temperature control (-25°C to +15°C)
  • Energy-efficient compressor system
  • PUF insulated panels for superior thermal insulation
  • Digital monitoring system
  • Multiple chamber options
  • Customizable capacity from 5 MT to 500 MT
