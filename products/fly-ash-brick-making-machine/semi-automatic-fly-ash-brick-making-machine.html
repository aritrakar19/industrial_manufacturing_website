Overview

The Semi Automatic Fly-Ash Brick Making Machine combines manual control with mechanical assistance for medium-scale brick production. With a hydraulic press system and vibration compaction, it produces stronger, more uniform bricks at higher volumes than manual machines. Ideal for growing businesses that want to scale production while keeping costs manageable.

Manufactured by Swarnabha Industries, this machine bridges the gap between manual and fully automatic systems. It offers the reliability of hydraulic pressing with operator-controlled production cycles, giving you the flexibility to optimize output based on demand while maintaining consistent brick quality throughout every batch.

Key Features

  • Hydraulic press system for uniform compression
  • Vibration compaction for higher density
  • Semi-automatic operation — minimal labor required
  • Higher production capacity than manual machines
  • Adjustable pressure settings for different brick types
  • Low power consumption for cost-efficient operation
