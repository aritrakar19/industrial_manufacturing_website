Overview

The Fully Automatic Fly-Ash Brick Making Machine is Swarnabha Industries' flagship brick production solution. With PLC-controlled automation, hydraulic systems, and continuous production capability, it delivers maximum output with minimal human intervention. Perfect for large-scale manufacturing operations seeking high ROI.

Engineered for industrial-grade performance, this machine features an advanced control system that automates the entire brick-making process — from raw material feeding to compression and ejection. With production capacities of up to 15,000 bricks per day, it is the ultimate choice for businesses aiming to dominate the construction materials market.
