Chowmein Noodles Making Machine

Our Chowmein Noodles Making Machine is a comprehensive automated production line designed for manufacturing chowmein, instant noodles, and pasta at commercial scale. The complete system includes dough mixing, sheet rolling, cutting, and steaming/drying stages, delivering consistent, high-quality noodle products from raw ingredients to finished packaging.

Built with food-grade stainless steel construction throughout, this machine meets the highest hygiene and safety standards for food production. The adjustable noodle thickness and multiple cutting options allow you to produce a diverse range of noodle products to meet varying market demands and consumer preferences.

Key Features

  • Complete Production Line – Integrated system covering dough mixing, sheeting, cutting, steaming, and drying in one unit.
  • Adjustable Noodle Thickness – Variable roller settings for thin, medium, or thick noodles to suit different product types.
  • Multiple Noodle Shapes – Interchangeable cutting dies for round, flat, square, and custom noodle profiles.
  • Stainless Steel Construction – 100% food-grade stainless steel for hygienic production and easy cleaning.
  • High Capacity Output – Produces up to 500 Kg of noodles per hour for large-scale commercial manufacturing.
  • Easy to Clean – Smooth surfaces and quick-disconnect components make sanitization fast and thorough.
