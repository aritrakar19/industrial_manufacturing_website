Melamine Plate Making Machine

Our Melamine Plate Making Machine is a high-performance hydraulic compression molding system specifically designed for producing melamine plates, bowls, cups, and tableware. Utilizing high temperature and pressure molding technology, this machine transforms melamine powder into durable, glossy, and visually appealing products that meet the highest quality standards.

Melamine tableware is in high demand across households, restaurants, hotels, and catering services due to its durability, heat resistance, and attractive appearance. Our machine enables manufacturers to produce premium-quality melamine products at competitive costs with consistent quality across every batch.

Key Features

  • High-Pressure Compression Molding – Up to 400 tons of press force for dense, durable melamine products with superior finish.
  • Precise Temperature Control – Advanced heating system maintains uniform temperature up to 180°C for consistent curing.
  • Multiple Cavity Molds – Multi-cavity mold capability for producing several pieces simultaneously per cycle.
  • Fast Cycle Time – Optimized pressing and curing cycles for high production throughput of 200-600 pieces per hour.
  • Consistent Product Quality – Precision-engineered hydraulic system ensures uniform pressure distribution across every product.
  • Easy Operation – User-friendly PLC control panel with programmable recipes for different product types.
