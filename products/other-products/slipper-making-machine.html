Slipper Making Machine

Our Slipper Making Machine is a robust hydraulic press system designed for producing EVA, PVC, and PU slippers with high precision and consistent quality. Featuring advanced mold alignment technology and powerful press force, this machine delivers perfectly shaped slippers with every cycle, making it ideal for footwear manufacturers of all scales.

The machine accommodates a wide range of slipper designs, sizes, and materials, giving manufacturers the flexibility to produce diverse product lines. With fast cycle times and durable construction, it maximizes production output while maintaining excellent product quality throughout long production runs.

Key Features

  • Hydraulic Press System – Powerful hydraulic press delivers uniform force for consistent slipper formation every time.
  • Multiple Material Compatibility – Works with EVA, PVC, and PU materials for diverse footwear product lines.
  • Precision Mold Alignment – Advanced alignment system ensures exact mold registration for flawless slipper shapes.
  • Fast Cycle Time – Rapid press cycles enable production of 100 to 500 pairs per hour.
  • Easy Mold Change – Quick mold changeover system for rapid switching between sizes and designs.
  • Durable Construction – Heavy-duty steel frame and premium hydraulic components for years of reliable service.
