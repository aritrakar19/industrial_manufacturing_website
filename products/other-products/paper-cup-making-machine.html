Paper Cup Making Machine

Our Paper Cup Making Machine is an advanced automated system designed for high-speed production of paper cups suitable for both hot and cold beverages. Using PE-coated paper, this machine creates leak-proof cups with precise forming, sealing, and bottom punching in a single continuous operation.

With growing awareness about environmental sustainability and the ban on single-use plastics, paper cups have become the preferred choice across the food and beverage industry. This machine enables you to produce high-quality, branded paper cups at competitive costs, making it an excellent business opportunity.

Key Features

  • High-Speed Cup Forming – Produces 40 to 120 cups per minute for efficient large-scale manufacturing.
  • Ultrasonic Sealing – Advanced ultrasonic technology creates strong, leak-proof seals without adhesives.
  • PE-Coated Paper Compatible – Works with single and double PE-coated paper for hot and cold beverage cups.
  • Multiple Cup Sizes – Adjustable tooling supports cup sizes from 2oz espresso cups to 16oz large cups.
  • Automatic Bottom Punching – Integrated bottom forming and sealing for a complete cup in one pass.
  • Low Wastage – Precision cutting and forming minimizes raw material waste for cost efficiency.

Frequently Asked Questions

The machine uses PE (polyethylene) coated paper, which provides a waterproof barrier for leak-proof cups. Single PE-coated paper is used for hot cups, while double PE-coated paper is recommended for cold beverage cups.

Yes, you can use pre-printed PE-coated paper to produce custom branded cups with logos, designs, and promotional messages. This opens up additional revenue streams through B2B custom cup orders.

Ultrasonic sealing uses high-frequency vibrations to bond the PE coating layers together, resulting in stronger, cleaner seals. Hot air sealing uses heated air to melt and bond the PE layers. Both methods produce leak-proof cups, but ultrasonic sealing is preferred for higher speed and better seal quality.
