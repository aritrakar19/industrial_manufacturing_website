Product Overview

The Horizontal Injection Molding Machine by Swarnabha Industries is designed for high-volume, precision plastic manufacturing. With a horizontal clamping system, these machines deliver excellent repeatability and are the industry standard for producing a wide range of plastic products from small components to large industrial parts.

Key Features

  • Horizontal Clamping System for high-volume production
  • High-speed injection for faster cycle times
  • Advanced hydraulic/servo system for energy savings
  • Large mold capacity for versatile applications
  • User-friendly PLC control panel
  • Robust construction for industrial durability
