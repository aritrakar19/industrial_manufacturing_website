Product Overview

The PET Series Injection Molding Machine from Swarnabha Industries is specifically designed for manufacturing PET preforms used in bottle production. With precise temperature control and high-speed injection, these machines ensure consistent quality preforms for the beverage, water, and packaging industries.

Key Features

  • Specialized PET processing capability
  • High-speed injection with precise control
  • Energy-efficient servo hydraulic system
  • Multi-cavity mold compatibility
  • Advanced drying and dehumidifying integration
  • Low acetaldehyde generation
