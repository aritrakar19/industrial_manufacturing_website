Raffia Recycling Machine

The Raffia Recycling Machine processes used PP/HDPE woven sacks, raffia bags, and jumbo bags into reusable plastic granules. With an integrated cutting, melting, and pelletizing system, it efficiently converts bulky raffia waste into compact granules ready for remanufacturing. Ideal for recyclers dealing with agricultural bags, cement bags, and industrial packaging waste.

Swarnabha Industries designs and manufactures Raffia Recycling Machines that deliver high throughput with minimal energy consumption. Our machines are built to handle the unique challenges of processing woven and laminated materials, ensuring consistent pellet quality batch after batch.

Key Features

  • Processes PP/HDPE woven sacks and raffia bags
  • Integrated cutter-compactor for efficient size reduction
  • Efficient degassing system removes moisture and volatiles
  • High output with low energy consumption
  • Handles printed and laminated raffia materials
  • Consistent pellet quality for remanufacturing
