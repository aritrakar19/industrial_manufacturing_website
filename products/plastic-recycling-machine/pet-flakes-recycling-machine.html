PET Flakes Recycling Machine

The PET Flakes Recycling Machine is designed for processing used PET bottles into clean, reusable PET flakes. This complete recycling line includes bottle sorting, label removal, crushing, hot washing, friction washing, and drying. The output PET flakes can be used for making polyester fiber, PET sheets, strapping bands, and new PET bottles.

Swarnabha Industries offers a fully integrated PET recycling solution that delivers high-purity flakes suitable for demanding downstream applications. Our machines are engineered for reliability and continuous operation, making them the preferred choice for PET recyclers across India.
