Plastic Dana Making Machine (Granule Making Machine)

The Plastic Dana Making Machine (Granule Making Machine) converts waste plastic into reusable plastic granules (dana). This machine is essential for the plastic recycling industry, transforming scrap plastic materials into valuable raw materials that can be reused in manufacturing. Built with heavy-duty components and advanced technology, our machines deliver consistent output with minimal waste.

Swarnabha Industries manufactures high-performance Plastic Dana Making Machines that are trusted by recyclers across India. Our machines are designed for continuous operation, ensuring maximum productivity and profitability for your recycling business.

Frequently Asked Questions

Our Plastic Dana Making Machine can process a wide range of thermoplastic materials including PP (Polypropylene), PE (Polyethylene), HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene), and LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene). This versatility allows you to handle various waste streams from different sources.

The machine produces uniform, high-quality plastic granules that are suitable for reuse in manufacturing processes. The automatic temperature control and precision screw design ensure consistent granule size and quality, making them ideal for injection molding, blow molding, and extrusion applications.

Power consumption varies based on the model and capacity selected. Our machines range from 10 HP to 50 HP motors. The energy-efficient design minimizes electricity usage while maintaining high output, helping you keep operational costs low and maximize profitability.

Most of our customers recover their investment within 6 to 12 months depending on the scale of operation and raw material availability. The high output capacity combined with low operational costs makes the Plastic Dana Making Machine a highly profitable investment in the recycling industry.

The machine is designed for low maintenance. Regular upkeep includes periodic lubrication of moving parts, cleaning of the barrel and screw, and inspection of heating elements. Swarnabha Industries provides a complete maintenance guide and our service team is available for on-site support when needed.
