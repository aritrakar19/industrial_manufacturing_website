HDPE Blow Molding Machine

Our HDPE Blow Molding Machine is a specialized extrusion blow molding system designed for producing high-density polyethylene containers, drums, and jerry cans. Utilizing advanced extrusion blow molding technology, this machine handles thick-walled products with consistent quality, making it the go-to solution for industrial container manufacturing.

Engineered for durability and precision, the HDPE blow molding machine features a robust hydraulic clamping system and automatic parison controller that ensures uniform wall distribution across every product. Whether you need small 100ml bottles or large 200-liter drums, this versatile machine delivers reliable results with every cycle.

Key Features

  • Extrusion Blow Molding Technology – Continuous extrusion process ideal for producing thick-walled hollow products from HDPE material.
  • Handles Thick-Walled Products – Capable of producing heavy-duty containers, drums, and tanks with consistent wall thickness.
  • Multi-Layer Capability – Supports multi-layer co-extrusion for enhanced product performance and barrier properties.
  • Hydraulic Clamping System – Powerful and precise clamping ensures secure mold closure and consistent product quality.
  • Auto Parison Controller – Automatic parison programming for precise wall thickness distribution throughout the product.
  • Heavy-Duty Construction – Built with premium-grade steel and components for years of reliable industrial use.
